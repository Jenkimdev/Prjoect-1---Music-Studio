Voted best music studio in wales 2 years running by Wales Independant Music Awards!


The owner of Made Up Studio is high profile producer John Appleseed. Born in Sweden in 1985, he moved to the UK when he was just 16. He has grown to become one of Wales' leading producers in the music industry, having been involved in the production of albums on behalf of several high profile music industry professionals with some of the finest talent. From Adele to Oasis, Mr Appleseed has worked with them all!
The studio was founded by Mr John Appleseed in 2012 and has slowly grown to become the number 1 music production studio in Wales, being home to some of the finest talent the Welsh Capital has seen in years. The facilities are second to none in Wales and there are 3 different studio rooms, equipped with all you need to get your tracks recorded, all for a reasonable price!
